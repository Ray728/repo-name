Mastering Prototypes: Avoiding Common Mistakes

  • Not testing early enough, risking major design flaws
  • Ignoring user feedback, missing valuable improvement opportunities
  • Over-engineering prototypes, wasting resources on non-critical features
  • Underestimating the importance of visual aesthetics and user experience

Avoid these pitfalls to unlock the full potential of your prototypes and optimize the success of your projects.
